Services we offer in our facilities.

Preventive care:
Teeth cleanings and examinations, dental sealants, fluoride treatments, and oral hygiene education.
Restorative care:
Tooth-colored fillings, dental crowns and bridges, dentures and partial dentures, dental implants.
Cosmetic dentistry:
Porcelain veneers, dental bonding, teeth whitening.
Orthodontics:
Braces and aligners to straighten teeth and improve bite.
Periodontics:
Treatment of gum disease.
Endodontics:
Root canal treatment.
Emergency dentistry:
Treatment for toothaches, broken teeth, and other dental emergencies.
Sedation dentistry:
For patients who are anxious or have difficulty cooperating with dental procedures.
Sleep apnea treatment:
For patients who suffer from sleep apnea, a sleep disorder that can cause serious health problems.
Sports dentistry:
Custom-made mouthguards to protect the teeth and gums during sports activities.
Children's dentistry:
Comprehensive dental care for children of all ages.
